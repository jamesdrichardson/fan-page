Who is Ahsoka?

Ahsoka is a great Jedi warrior in the Star Wars univers. Ahsoka is also the name of my cat. Both have a lot of the same characteristics and that's why I thought it was a good idea to make this joint fan page! so share your thoughts, share your pictures, and share your stories; and together we can make a great fan page!

You can share things about either Ahsoka or even another Ahsoka who may be in your life! We're just happy to have you here.
